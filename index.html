All About Liverpool FC

  • Facts

  • Liverpool FC play in Liverpool, England, the same city the Beatles started in!
  • The club was founded in 1982
  • Liverpool have 2 huge rivals: Everton FC, who play less than 1 mile away from Liverpool, and Manchester United, who are the second most successful club in England
  • The fan base is widely considered to be the most passionate in England, with each home game creating an electric atmosphere
